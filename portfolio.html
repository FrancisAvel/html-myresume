Gearbox Shaft and Gear Design

This project was conducted as group project for the Design of Machine Elements Unit. Gear box and output rpm of the gearbox was provided. With this information, stress calculations considering axial and torsional loading were carried out. The shaft, gear and bearing type material was then selected based on the stresses calculated with safety factor inclusion.
